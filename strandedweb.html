This doesn't feel like the real experience...

Sadly despite best efforts in optimization, many corners had to be cut: the main menu video is now a static image, images appear blurry while assets are loading, music takes a while to play and sound effects may not play in time. To avoid these issues, we recommend that you download and play a local version of the game rather than in a web browser; this version is for players who have no local file saving or application running permissions. (...or who don't want to update.)
